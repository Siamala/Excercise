Build Video Rental program acc to following requirements
1. Price for first rental day of standard movie is 1,5 zł. For every standard movie rental day 1 regular customer point is assigned.
2. Second and thrid rental day of standard movie is free and no regular customer points are assigned
3. For every next rental day 1,5 zł is charged and 1 regular customer point is assigned
4. Movies for childs are rented for 1 zł per day and 1 regular customer point is assigned for a movie
5. There should be a possibility to calculate price and regular customer points for more than one movie
